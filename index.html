What is QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of matrix barcode that can store data in two dimensions. It is widely used for encoding information such as URLs, text, and contact details for quick access using a QR code scanner or smartphone camera.

Why Use QR Codes?

QR codes are versatile and allow users to quickly access information or perform actions like visiting a website, saving contact information, or making payments. They are highly efficient in bridging the gap between offline and online experiences.

Benefits of QR Codes

  • Fast and easy to use with smartphones.
  • Can store a large amount of data in a compact format.
  • Highly customizable and suitable for branding purposes.
  • Promotes contactless interactions, making them ideal for digital payments and sharing information.
  • Improves user engagement and drives traffic to online resources.
